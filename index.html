About Rockstar Academi Islamic School

Producing Future Leaders

Rockstar Academi Islamic School is a modern international Islamic school designed to produce future leaders who are strong in faith, excellent in knowledge, and ready to compete globally. Our school integrates:

  • Islamic Education
  • International Curriculum
  • Leadership Development
  • Character Building
  • Technology Education

Owner & Founder

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a world-class Islamic educational institution that produces Qur'anic, intelligent, and globally competitive leaders.

Our Mission

  • Develop strong Islamic character based on the Qur'an and Sunnah
  • Provide international standard education
  • Build leadership and entrepreneurship skills
  • Integrate Islamic values with science and technology
  • Create future global Muslim leaders
