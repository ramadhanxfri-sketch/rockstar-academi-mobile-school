Integrated International Islamic Curriculum

Blending world-class academic standards with profound Islamic values.

Our Holistic Approach

At Rockstar Academi, we provide a balanced education that addresses the intellectual, spiritual, and physical needs of our students. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for the complexities of the 21st century while keeping them rooted in their faith.

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive studies in Fiqh, Aqidah, Akhlaq, and Islamic History. We focus on applying Islamic teachings to contemporary challenges and daily life.

Tahfizh Program

A rigorous yet nurturing memorization program with a target of 10-15 Juz by graduation. We emphasize proper Tajweed and understanding (Tafsir).

Target: 10 - 15 Juz Graduation

Arabic & English Immersion

Students achieve trilingual mastery. Arabic for religious understanding and English for global academic excellence, alongside Bahasa Indonesia.

STEM Education

Integrating Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. We use inquiry-based learning to foster critical thinking and innovation.

Leadership Development

Structured programs based on the Sunnah. Students learn ethical decision-making, public speaking, and community service.

Character Building

Nurturing Adab (manners) and Akhlaq (character). We believe that knowledge without character is incomplete.

Academic Framework

Cambridge & National Standards

Our curriculum is mapped against the Cambridge International Education (CIE) standards for English, Math, and Science, combined with the Indonesian National Curriculum requirements. This dual approach ensures our students are globally competitive and locally grounded.

  • International IGCSE & A-Level Preparation
  • Integrated STEM & Islamic Labs
  • Inquiry-Based Project Learning
  • Holistic Assessment Systems

Progressive Assessment

We use a variety of assessment methods to track student progress, focusing not just on test scores but on their development in character, leadership, and religious understanding.
