Special Programs

Enhancing student potential through specialized Islamic and modern programs.

Tahfizh Excellence Program

Nurturing the Guardians of the Qur'an

Our Tahfizh program is designed for students who show exceptional dedication to memorizing the Holy Qur'an. We provide a specialized track that focuses on intensive memorization while maintaining academic standards.

Objectives

  • Memorize the Qur'an with correct Tajweed and Makharij.
  • Develop a deep understanding of Qur'anic meanings (Tafsir).
  • Embody Qur'anic character in daily life.

Benefits for Students

  • Individualized memorization plans.
  • Certification upon graduation.
  • Participation in local and international competitions.

Activities

  • Arabic & English Debate Club.
  • International Student Exchange.
  • Global Pen-pal initiatives.

International Language Program

Mastery of English and Arabic

Communication is the key to global leadership. Our language program ensures that students are not only fluent in Arabic for religious studies but also proficient in English for international communication and academic success.

Objectives

  • Achieve native-level fluency in English.
  • Master Arabic for classical and modern contexts.
  • Develop trilingual proficiency alongside Bahasa Indonesia.

STEM Technology Program

Future-Ready Innovation

We integrate modern technology and scientific inquiry with Islamic philosophy. Our STEM program prepares students for the challenges of the future while remaining grounded in their faith.

Activities

  • Robotics and Coding workshops.
  • Environmental science projects.
  • Integrated STEM fairs and competitions.

Benefits for Students

  • Access to state-of-the-art computer labs.
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
  • Foundation for future careers in tech and science.

Core Focus

  • Public speaking & rhetoric.
  • Ethical decision-making.
  • Community service & project management.

Islamic Leadership Program

Leaders Following the Sunnah

True leadership is about service and responsibility. Our program draws from the life of the Prophet (PBUH) to teach students how to lead with integrity, compassion, and vision.

Objectives

  • Develop self-confidence and moral courage.
  • Learn practical leadership skills through workshops.
  • Inspire a sense of responsibility towards the Ummah.
