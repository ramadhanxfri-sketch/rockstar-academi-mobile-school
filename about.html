About Rockstar Academi Islamic School

Building a generation with Qur'anic values and global excellence since our foundation.

Our History

The Journey of Excellence

Rockstar Academi Islamic School was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional Islamic values and modern international education. Starting from a small community initiative, we have grown into a prestigious institution known for our commitment to excellence, character building, and academic rigor.

Our journey has been defined by our dedication to producing leaders who are not only intellectually capable but also spiritually grounded. We believe that education should empower the soul as much as the mind.

Our Purpose

Mission of the School

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that integrates Islamic teachings with the highest international standards, ensuring our students are prepared for success in this world and the hereafter.

Qur'anic Integration

We weave Qur'anic principles into every aspect of our curriculum, fostering a deep love and understanding of Allah's word.

Global Knowledge

Our students master international curricula, preparing them for top universities and global leadership roles.

Character Development

We focus on Akhlaq and leadership, nurturing compassionate, ethical, and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To become a world-class Islamic educational institution that produces Qur'anic, intelligent, and globally competitive leaders who inspire positive change in the world.

Core Values

  • Taqwa: Deep consciousness of Allah in all actions.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors.
  • Integrity: Honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and modern technology.
  • Service: Commitment to serving the community and humanity.

Owner & Founder

Putri Yulita

"Rockstar Academi Islamic School was born out of a desire to see our children grow with the strength of the Qur'an and the tools of the modern world. We are committed to providing an environment where faith and knowledge flourish together."

As the founder, Putri Yulita leads with a vision of excellence and a deep passion for Islamic education. Her leadership has been instrumental in establishing Rockstar Academi as a beacon of high-quality Islamic schooling.
