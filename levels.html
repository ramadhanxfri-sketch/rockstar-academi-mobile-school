Education Levels

Tailored learning paths from early childhood to secondary education.

Ages 2 - 4

Early Childhood

Our Early Childhood program focuses on nurturing the natural curiosity of children through play-based discovery. We introduce basic Islamic values and social skills in a warm, loving environment.

Learning Method: Play-based, Montessori-inspired Islamic learning.

Focus: Motor skills, social interaction, basic Dua and Surah.

Example Daily Schedule

TimeActivity
08:00 - 08:30Morning Circle & Dua
08:30 - 09:30Sensory Play & Discovery
09:30 - 10:00Healthy Snack Break
10:00 - 11:00Creative Arts & Stories

Ages 4 - 6

Kindergarten

In Kindergarten, we build a strong foundation for literacy, numeracy, and religious understanding. We encourage independent thinking and a love for the Arabic language.

Learning Method: Interactive learning with hands-on activities.

Focus: Phonics, basic math, Tahfizh (Juz Amma), Arabic vocabulary.

Example Daily Schedule

TimeActivity
07:30 - 08:15Tahfizh & Morning Adhkar
08:15 - 09:30Literacy & Numeracy
09:30 - 10:00Playtime & Snack
10:00 - 11:30Arabic & Islamic Studies

Ages 6 - 12

Primary School

Our Primary School integrates the International Cambridge Curriculum with intensive Tahfizh and Islamic studies. We aim to produce students who excel academically while embodying the character of the Prophet (PBUH).

Learning Method: Integrated STEM and Inquiry-based learning.

Focus: Advanced Literacy, STEM, 10-15 Juz Tahfizh, Character (Adab).

Example Daily Schedule

TimeActivity
07:00 - 08:30Tahfizh Intensive Session
08:30 - 12:00Core Subjects (English, Math, Science)
12:00 - 13:00Dhuhr Prayer & Lunch
13:00 - 14:30Islamic Studies & Arabic

Ages 12 - 18

Secondary School

Secondary education focuses on leadership, critical thinking, and global awareness. We prepare students for IGCSE and A-Levels while deepening their understanding of Fiqh and Islamic philosophy.

Learning Method: Project-based learning, Leadership seminars, STEM labs.

Focus: IGCSE/A-Level subjects, Islamic Leadership, Entrepreneurship.

Example Daily Schedule

TimeActivity
07:00 - 08:30Advanced Tahfizh & Tafsir
08:30 - 13:00Academic Lessons (STEM/Social Sciences)
13:00 - 14:00Dhuhr Prayer, Lunch & Mentoring
14:00 - 15:30Leadership & Extracurriculars
